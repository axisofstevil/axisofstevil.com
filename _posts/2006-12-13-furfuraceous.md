## "Furfuraceous" ##

fer·fer·ray·shuss; adj.

1. Consisting of or covered with flaky particles.
 
> "This morning, two East Shore, NE teenagers discovered a ***furfuraceous*** block of ice containing an ancient recreational apparatus."
