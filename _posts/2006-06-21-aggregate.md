## "Aggregate" ##

'a-gri-g&t; adj.,

1. formed by the collection of units or particles into a body, mass, or amount.
2. clustered.
 
> "The new KFC Cheesy Chicken Mashed Potato Bowls have inspired other ***aggregate*** fast food dinners."
