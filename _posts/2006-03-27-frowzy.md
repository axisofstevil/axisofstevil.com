## "frowzy" ##

(FROU-zee) adjective, also frowsy, frouzy.

1. Unkempt, slovenly.
2. Having a musty odor.
 
> "Franklin University realized their mascot, The Franklin ***Frowzy***, wasn't exactly crowd-friendly."
